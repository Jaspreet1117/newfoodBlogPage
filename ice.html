Ice Cream Recipe

This easy homemade ice cream recipe requires only a few ingredients and no ice cream maker. Perfect for a refreshing treat on a hot day!

Preparation Time

  • Prep: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Freezing: 4 hours

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Optional: chocolate chips, fruit, nuts, etc.

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the heavy cream with a hand mixer or stand mixer until stiff peaks form.
  2. Gently fold in the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Add any optional ingredients like chocolate chips or fruit and mix gently.
  4. Pour the mixture into a loaf pan or freezer-safe container and cover with plastic wrap.
  5. Freeze for at least 4 hours, or until the ice cream is firm. Serve and enjoy!
