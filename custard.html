Fruit Custard Recipe..

A creamy and delicious dessert, perfect for any occasion.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 30 minutes

Ingredients

For the Custard

  • 1 liter milk
  • 100g sugar
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the Fruit Topping

  • Assorted fresh fruits (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Custard:
    1. Heat the milk in a saucepan over medium heat until it starts to simmer.
    2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, sugar, cornstarch, and vanilla extract until smooth.
    3. Slowly pour the hot milk into the egg mixture, whisking constantly.
    4. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until the custard thickens.
    5. Remove from heat and strain the custard to remove any lumps.
  2. Prepare the Fruit Topping:
    1. In a bowl, combine the fresh fruits, sugar, and lemon juice.
    2. Toss gently to coat the fruits.
  3. Assemble the Dessert:
    1. Pour the custard into individual serving bowls.
    2. Top each serving with a generous amount of the fruit mixture.
  4. Serve Chilled:
    1. Chill the dessert in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours before serving.
