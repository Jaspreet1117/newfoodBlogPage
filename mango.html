Mango Milkshake Recipe

This delicious mango milkshake is creamy, refreshing, and perfect for a hot day. Made with fresh mangoes and milk, it's a simple yet flavorful treat.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: Approximately 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large ripe mango, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup cold milk
  • 1-2 tbsp sugar or honey (optional, adjust to taste)
  • 3-4 ice cubes
  • Optional: 1 scoop vanilla ice cream for extra creaminess

Instructions

  1. In a blender, add the chopped mango, milk, sugar or honey, and ice cubes.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. If desired, add a scoop of vanilla ice cream and blend again for extra creaminess.
  4. Pour into a glass, garnish with small mango pieces or a sprinkle of nuts, and serve chilled.
