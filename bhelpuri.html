Bhel Puri Recipe

A tangy and spicy Indian street food snack...

Preparation Time

  • Prep: 10 minutes
  • Cook: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup puffed rice (puri)
  • 1/2 cup sev (fried vermicelli)
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black salt
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Yogurt, for serving

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Ingredients: Chop the onions, tomatoes, and coriander leaves.
  2. Mix the Ingredients: In a large bowl, combine the puffed rice, sev, chopped vegetables, chaat masala, red chili powder, and black salt.
  3. Add the Flavorings: Squeeze the lemon juice over the mixture and toss well to coat all the ingredients.
  4. Serve: Serve the Bhel Puri immediately, garnished with yogurt.
