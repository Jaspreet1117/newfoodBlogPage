Classic Pizza Recipe

This classic pizza recipe has a deliciously crispy crust topped with melty cheese, tangy tomato sauce, and your favorite toppings.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: 20 minutes
  • Resting Time: 1 hour
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 2 and 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast

For the Toppings

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 and 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup sliced olives
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian herbs (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Dough: In a small bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil, then mix to form a dough. Knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth.
  3. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  4. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a baking sheet or pizza stone inside to heat up.
  5. Shape the Dough: Punch down the dough, then roll or stretch it into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a lightly floured pizza peel or baking sheet.
  6. Add Toppings: Spread pizza sauce over the dough, sprinkle with mozzarella cheese, and add bell peppers, mushrooms, olives, and any other toppings you like.
  7. Bake the Pizza: Carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated baking sheet or stone. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, top with fresh basil if desired, and serve hot!
