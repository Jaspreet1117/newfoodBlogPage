Sandwich Recipe

This easy and quick sandwich is perfect for any meal. Customize it with your favorite fillings for a satisfying snack or meal.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: Approximately 5 minutes
  • Cooking: None required

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter or mayonnaise
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 slice of ham or turkey
  • Optional: lettuce, tomato, cucumber, etc.

Instructions

  1. Spread butter or mayonnaise on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese, ham or turkey, and any optional veggies between the bread slices.
  3. Press the slices together, cut in half if desired, and serve.

Calories200 kcal
Protein8g
Carbs20g

Tomato Soup

Tomato Soup Recipe

A comforting and flavorful soup.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: 10 minutes
  • Cook: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Saut√© the Vegetables: Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, cook until softened.
  2. Add Tomatoes and Spices: Add chopped tomatoes, oregano, black pepper, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the tomatoes have softened.
  3. Add Broth and Simmer: Pour in vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the soup has thickened.
  4. Blend the Soup: Using an immersion blender or a regular blender, blend the soup until smooth.
  5. Add Cream (Optional): Stir in heavy cream, if desired.
  6. Serve: Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.

Carbs15g

Vada Pav Recipe

A popular Maharashtrian street food.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: 30 minutes
  • Cook: 20 minutes

Ingredients

For the Vada:

  • 1 cup urad dal (soaked overnight)
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

For the Pav:

  • Pav buns

For the Green Chutney:

  • 1 bunch coriander leaves
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

Making the Vada:

  1. Grind the soaked urad dal into a fine paste.
  2. Add the green chili, ginger-garlic paste, cumin seeds, red chili powder, and salt to the batter. Mix well.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Drop spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown.
  4. Drain the vadas on paper towels.

Making the Green Chutney:

  1. Blend the coriander leaves, green chilies, cumin seeds, lemon juice, salt, and water into a smooth paste.

Assembling the Vada Pav:

  1. Cut the pav buns in half.
  2. Apply a generous amount of green chutney to one half of the bun.
  3. Place a hot vada on top of the chutney.
  4. Cover with the other half of the bun.
