Coffee Recipe

A classic and invigorating beverage.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: 5 minutes
  • Brew: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons coffee grounds
  • 1-2 cups water
  • Milk or cream (optional)
  • Sugar (optional)

Instructions

  1. *Boil Water:* Boil the desired amount of water.
  2. *Brew the Coffee:* Pour the hot water over the coffee grounds in a coffee filter or French press. Let it brew for 4-5 minutes.
  3. *Add Milk and Sugar:* Add milk or cream and sugar to taste.
  4. *Serve Hot:* Serve the coffee hot.
