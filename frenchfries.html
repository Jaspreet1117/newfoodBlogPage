French Fries Recipe

Crispy and delicious, perfect for a snack or side dish.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: 10 minutes
  • Cook: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into 1/2-inch thick sticks
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak the Potatoes: Soak the cut potatoes in cold water for 30 minutes to remove excess starch.
  2. Dry the Potatoes: Drain the potatoes and pat them dry with a clean kitchen towel.
  3. Heat the Oil: Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 375°F (190°C).
  4. Fry the Potatoes: Fry the potatoes in batches for 3-4 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  5. Season the Fries: Season the fries with salt and black pepper to taste.
  6. Serve Hot: Serve the hot, crispy French fries immediately.
