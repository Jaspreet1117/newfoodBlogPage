Sandwich Recipe

This easy and quick sandwich is perfect for any meal. Customize it with your favorite fillings for a satisfying snack or meal.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: Approximately 5 minutes
  • Cooking: None required

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter or mayonnaise
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • 1 slice of ham or turkey
  • Optional: lettuce, tomato, cucumber, etc.

Instructions

  1. Spread butter or mayonnaise on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place cheese, ham or turkey, and any optional veggies between the bread slices.
  3. Press the slices together, cut in half if desired, and serve.
