Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This omelette is a classic, with eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your preferred ingredients.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Optional: cheese, ham, vegetables, etc.

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl, adding salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan, letting them cook undisturbed for a minute.
  4. Add optional fillings, then fold and serve.
