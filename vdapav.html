Vada Pav Recipe

A popular Maharashtrian street food.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: 30 minutes
  • Cook: 20 minutes

Ingredients

For the Vada:

  • 1 cup urad dal (soaked overnight)
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

For the Pav:

  • Pav buns

For the Green Chutney:

  • 1 bunch coriander leaves
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed

Instructions

Making the Vada:

  1. Grind the soaked urad dal into a fine paste.
  2. Add the green chili, ginger-garlic paste, cumin seeds, red chili powder, and salt to the batter. Mix well.
  3. Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Drop spoonfuls of batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown.
  4. Drain the vadas on paper towels.

Making the Green Chutney:

  1. Blend the coriander leaves, green chilies, cumin seeds, lemon juice, salt, and water into a smooth paste.

Assembling the Vada Pav:

  1. Cut the pav buns in half.
  2. Apply a generous amount of green chutney to one half of the bun.
  3. Place a hot vada on top of the chutney.
  4. Cover with the other half of the bun.
