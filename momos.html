Momos Recipe

A delicious and popular Tibetan dumpling.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: 30 minutes
  • Cook: 15 minutes

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Water as needed

For the Filling:

  • 500g minced meat (chicken, pork, or vegetable)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, minced
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste

For the Dipping Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon red chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 green chili, minced

Instructions

Making the Dough:

  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl.
  2. Gradually add water and knead into a smooth dough.
  3. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.

Making the Filling:

  1. In a bowl, combine minced meat, onion, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, black pepper, and salt.
  2. Mix well and set aside.

Assembling the Momos:

  1. Roll out the dough into thin sheets.
  2. Cut out circles using a cookie cutter.
  3. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle.
  4. Fold the dough over the filling and seal the edges.
  5. Steam the momos for 10-15 minutes, or until cooked through.

Making the Dipping Sauce:

  1. In a small bowl, combine soy sauce, vinegar, red chili sauce, sugar, garlic, and green chili.
  2. Mix well and serve with the momos.
