Pav Bhaji Recipe

A popular Indian street food.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: 30 minutes
  • Cook: 45 minutes

Ingredients

For the Bhaji:

  • 4 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 cup water
  • Coriander leaves, for garnish

For the Pav:

  • Pav buns
  • Butter, for toasting

Instructions

  1. Make the Bhaji:
    1. Heat butter in a large pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
    2. Add chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger-garlic paste. Saut√© until the onions turn golden brown.
    3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft and mushy.
    4. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.
    5. Add the mashed potatoes and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the flavors are well-combined.
    6. Garnish with coriander leaves.
  2. Toast the Pav: Cut the pav buns in half and toast them on a griddle or in a toaster.
  3. Serve: Serve the hot bhaji with toasted pav buns. Add a dollop of butter to the pav for extra flavor.
