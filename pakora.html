Pakora Recipe

A crispy and delicious Indian snack.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: 20 minutes
  • Cook: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for frying
  • Vegetables of your choice (e.g., onion, potato, capsicum, spinach)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Batter: In a bowl, mix besan, turmeric powder, red chili powder, cumin seeds, garam masala, salt, and water to form a smooth batter.
  2. Prepare the Vegetables: Cut the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Dip and Fry: Dip the vegetable pieces into the batter and deep fry in hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Serve Hot: Serve the hot pakoras with green chutney and tamarind chutney.
