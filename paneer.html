Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

This delicious Paneer Butter Masala is a popular Indian dish, featuring soft paneer cubes in a creamy tomato-based gravy, perfect to enjoy with naan or rice.

Preparation Time

  • Prep: Approximately 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and 1 tbsp butter in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add tomato puree, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates.
  4. Add cream, garam masala, and the remaining butter. Stir well to combine and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add paneer cubes and gently mix to coat them in the gravy. Simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
